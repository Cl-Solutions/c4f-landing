# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

B2B primary: GaLaBau professionals, municipalities, golf course managers, nurseries (Raiffeisen network). Secondary: conscious home gardeners and balcony growers. They evaluate products practically — they want evidence, not poetry. They're outdoors, at a desk, or on a tablet in the field.

## Product Purpose

Carbon4Future sells activated biochar soil-amendment products. The site exists to convert skeptical professionals into first-time buyers by making the science feel credible and the environmental benefit feel real. Success = inquiry or purchase intent.

## Brand Personality

Geerdet, wissenschaftlich, warm, zukunftsorientiert, nachhaltig. The brand speaks like a knowledgeable colleague who genuinely cares about soil health — not a startup, not a pharma company, not a weekend eco-hobbyist brand.

## Anti-references

- Premium dark tech (Vercel, Linear) — too cold, too startup
- Generic bio/organic market aesthetic — too hippie, not credible enough
- Cheap garden center design — undermines the science
- Pharmaceutical clinical white — too sterile, wrong register

## Design Principles

1. **Prove it** — science and numbers before adjectives. Let data carry the weight.
2. **Earned warmth** — warmth through craft and precision, not decoration or soft gradients.
3. **Made in Germany as substance** — not a flag sticker. Short supply chains, real processes, no greenwashing language.
4. **Grounded in the physical** — soil, roots, carbon, earth. Images and metaphors stay tactile.
5. **Confident restraint** — the brand knows its product works. No overselling, no urgency tricks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion respected via `prefers-reduced-motion`. All form controls labelled.
